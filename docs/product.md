# 1. Vision

GamePitch AI is an AI-powered marketing assistant built specifically for independent game developers and small studios.

Many developers excel at creating games but struggle to communicate their value effectively. Marketing often becomes a bottleneck due to limited experience, time, or budget.

GamePitch AI bridges that gap by generating high-quality marketing content tailored to the video game industry, allowing developers to focus on building great games instead of learning copywriting or hiring expensive marketing professionals.

The long-term vision is to evolve GamePitch AI into a complete AI-powered marketing platform for game developers.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Independent game developers face significant challenges when promoting their games.

Although they may create technically impressive products, many lack the marketing knowledge required to present them effectively to potential players or publishers.

Hiring professional marketers or copywriters is often too expensive for solo developers and small studios, resulting in:

- Poor Steam store pages
- Low social media engagement
- Weak publisher pitches
- Reduced visibility
- Lower conversion rates

Existing AI assistants are general-purpose tools that require extensive prompt engineering and rarely understand the specific language, platforms, and expectations of the game development industry.

GamePitch AI solves this problem by providing AI-generated marketing content specifically designed for game developers.

# 4. Value Proposition

GamePitch AI enables developers to generate professional marketing assets in seconds instead of hours.

Unlike generic AI assistants, GamePitch AI focuses exclusively on the game development industry and produces content optimized for real publishing platforms.

Core advantages include:

- Industry-specific prompt engineering
- Ready-to-use marketing content
- Minimal learning curve
- Fast generation times
- Production-ready outputs
- Designed to evolve into a scalable SaaS platform

---

# 5. Business Justification

The indie game market continues to grow every year, while effective marketing remains one of the biggest challenges for small development teams.

Most existing AI writing tools are designed for general content creation rather than video game marketing.

By focusing on a single niche, GamePitch AI can deliver significantly higher quality results than generic AI assistants while maintaining a simple user experience.

The product also establishes a strong foundation for future SaaS capabilities, including subscriptions, analytics, collaboration, premium prompt libraries, and additional marketing generators.

---

# 6. MVP Scope

The MVP intentionally focuses on solving one problem exceptionally well.

## Included

- Game information form
- Steam Description Generator
- AI-powered content generation
- Copy to clipboard
- Content regeneration

## Excluded

- Authentication
- Billing
- Persistent storage
- Team collaboration
- Analytics dashboard
- Multi-language support
- Multiple AI providers
- Mobile application

---

# 7. Success Metrics

The MVP will be considered successful if it meets the following objectives:

- Generate content in under five seconds
- Produce high-quality Steam descriptions
- Require minimal user input
- Provide a clean and intuitive user experience
- Demonstrate a production-ready backend architecture
- Be easily extensible for future generators

# 9. Product Principles

Every decision made during development should follow these principles:

- Solve a real problem.
- Prioritize user value over technical complexity.
- Keep the user experience simple.
- Build scalable foundations from day one.
- Prefer maintainability over clever implementations.
- Follow production-ready engineering practices.
- Design with extensibility in mind.
- Avoid unnecessary features during the MVP.

---

# 10. Out of Scope

The following features are intentionally excluded from the initial release:

- Full CRM capabilities
- Complete game publishing workflow
- Community management
- AI image generation
- AI video generation
- Marketplace integrations
- Publisher management
- Advanced analytics
- Enterprise features

These capabilities may be considered in future iterations after validating the core product.

---

# 11. Product Success Criteria

GamePitch AI will be considered successful when developers can generate professional marketing content within minutes, significantly reducing the effort required to promote their games while maintaining high output quality.

The project should demonstrate not only a valuable product but also production-grade software engineering practices, including clean architecture, scalability, maintainability, observability, security, automated testing, and continuous delivery.
